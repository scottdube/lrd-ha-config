Prior: **ADR-025 amendment correction — voltage trap framing was wrong, real root cause identified via fresh-board bench characterization.** Controlled PPK2 session on a stock unmodded XIAO ESP32-C6 disproved yesterday's voltage-trap diagnosis. A fresh C6 powered via 3V3 pin at 3.3 V achieves 15.66 µA sleep, exactly matching Seeed forum reports — there is no inherent voltage trap below 3.5 V on this chip. The 365 µA delta on the deployed modded float is fully explained by two independent costs: (1) **NTC voltage divider continuously powered from 3V3 — ~245 µA**, far more than passive divider math predicts (~35 µA) because the ADC pin's frontend at 12 dB attenuation magnifies current through the divider; (2) **ADC subsystem initialization bias — ~74 µA**, fixed chip-level cost of having the C6's SAR ADC peripheral configured in ESP-IDF, independent of channel count, attenuation, or pin state. Proven fix bench-validated: move the 47 kΩ NTC reference resistor's high side from 3V3 to a GPIO (GPIO2, RTC-capable, 20 mA drive >> 35 µA load), drive HIGH in `on_boot` priority-800 lambda, drive LOW + `gpio_hold_en` before `deep_sleep.enter`.   - **Per-circuit baseline-drift monitor.** Rolling 7-day vs 30-day median idle-state current per branch CT. Alert when a circuit's quiescent draw drifts upward >X% (threshold TBD per circuit class). Per-circuit visibility is the unique Vue contribution — Ting can't tell you *which* circuit.
  - **Sustained over/undervoltage logger.** Already partially captured via the L1/L2 voltage sensors; needs a 5-min rolling-average template + threshold-crossing automation that pushes via `notify.scott_and_ha`. Catches the slow utility-side problems (transformer drift, sustained 128-130V brown stretches) that Ting would also flag.
  - **Sub-second current excursion log (per circuit).** Optional — log every CT reading where instantaneous power exceeds 2× rolling-15-min median for >1 sample. Diagnostic record for intermittent AFCI/GFCI nuisance trips. Volume management may make this expensive in the recorder; defer until specific need.
  - **Appliance failure prediction (passive).** Use existing per-circuit data to track: pool pump current at fixed speed setpoints over weeks/months, HVAC compressor inrush current over time, heat-pump compressor steady-state amps under known load. No new sensors — pure template + statistics on what Vue is already publishing. Equipment-failure precursors, not fire hazards.
  - **Open decisions:** thresholds (need baseline data first), notify target (likely `notify.scott_and_ha`), whether to bundle as one ADR or split fire-safety-adjacent (imbalance, voltage) from equipment-health (baseline drift, appliance prediction).
- **Ting integration (separately tracked under "Integrations to evaluate").** Coexists with the Vue analytics layer; provides the high-frequency (30 MHz) RF-spectrum arc-detection that Vue (1 Hz RMS, 8 orders of magnitude slower) physically cannot replicate.
